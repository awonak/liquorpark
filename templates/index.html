Beer, Wine & Liquor

I had been working at Life's a Cabernet for about three years when my boss mentioned to me he was thinking about selling the shop in order to put more of his focus into a new business he’d started. I loved everything about the shop—what we sell, the community, and the customers—so to me this sounded like a great opportunity to take the helm.

In order to realize this dream, I reached out to some friends who each had a ton of experience in the different areas necessary to run a successful liquor boutique. Paul, an established member of the Chicago beer community whose knowledge of craft brews runs deep, was the first person I called. John, Paul’s father, is a second-generation entrepreneur and CPA who has started a number of successful businesses and also teaches entrepreneurship at Northern Illinois University’s MBA school. And Paul's brother Adam is a seasoned web developer and beer enthusiast, and I knew he’d be exactly the person to keep us up-to-date with our website and our inventory and sales systems.

We’re transforming Life’s a Cabernet into Liquor Park. I’m proud to work with this amazing team to bring truly special craft beers, wines, and spirits to our neighborhood.

Janel Syron
Sommelier and Proprietor, Liquor Park
