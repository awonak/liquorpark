About

At Liquor Park we’re passionate about thoughtful, carefully crafted beverages. By tapping our combined twenty years’ experience in the Chicago industry, we’ve curated a collection of cult-favorite beers, sought-after spirits, and small-batch wines from around the world, including artisan and made in the Midwest products you can’t get just anywhere. Our shelves are stocked and we’re ready to deliver spot-on pairings and killer recommendations for any palate, budget, or occasion.

Come find us in the heart of Wicker Park–Bucktown, just east of the intersection of North, Damen, and Milwaukee Avenues at 1937 West North Avenue. Email: shop@drinkliquorpark.com

Wine Club

The Wine Club is the best way to experience the wines that Liquor Park has to offer.

Each month the staff brings you wines that are hand selected, limited release and great finds. Some months we may bring you staff favorites or some wines that are a bit more esoteric to broaden your palate. As with any club there are a number of other benefits for becoming a member after you choose between one of the following options:

JV Membership $39.99/month

With this member pass the Liquor Park team brings you two wines that will normally retail for about $50-$60. Most wines we choose have a great story to tell, pair with the season, or are just a great find.

A-Team $89.99/month

This is a unique club. Think California Cult wines, Estate grown grapes that are limited production and limited distribution. Occasionally there maybe a bottle of unique scotch or whiskey instead of wine.

More BENEFITS upon JOINING THE LIQUOR PARK WINE CLUB

In addition to our great monthly wines, you will also receive:
-10% off everything in the shop... EVERY DAY!
-15% off (at least) all wines featured in either wine club that month
-15% off case purchases of wine.

Talk to us in store to sign up!
